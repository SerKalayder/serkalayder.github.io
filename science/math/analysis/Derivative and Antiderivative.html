Derivative and Antiderivative

Derivative

# \( f(x) \) \( f'(x) \)
1 \( C \) \( 0 \)
2 \( x \) \( 1 \)
3 \( x^2 \) \( 2x \)
4 \( x^n \) \( nx^{n-1} \)
5 \( \sqrt{x} \) \( \frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}} \)
6 \( \sqrt[n]{x^k} = x^{\frac{k}{n}} \) \( \frac{k}{n}x^{\frac{k}{n}-1} \)
7 \( \frac{1}{x^k} = x^{-k} \) \( -kx^{-k-1} \)
8 \( \sin{x} \) \( \cos{x} \)
9 \( \cos{x} \) \( -\sin{x} \)
10 \( \tan{x} \) \( \frac{1}{\cos^2{x}} \)
11 \( \cot{x} \) \( -\frac{1}{\sin^2{x}} \)
12 \( \arcsin{x} \) \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)
13 \( \arccos{x} \) \( -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \)
14 \( \arctan{x} \) \( \frac{1}{1+x^2} \)
15 \( \cot^{-1}{x} \) \( -\frac{1}{1+x^2} \)
16 \( e^x \) \( e^x \)
17 \( a^x \) \( a^x\ln{a} \)
18 \( \ln{x} \) \( \frac{1}{x} \)
19 \( \log_{a}{x} \) \( \frac{1}{x\ln{a}} \)
Differentiation rules
# Rule
1 \( (u \pm v)' = u' \pm v' \)
2 \( (uv)' = u'v + uv' \)
3 \( (cu)' = cu' \)
4 \( (\frac{u}{v})' = \frac{u'v-uv'}{v^2} \)
5 \( (uvw)' = u'vw+uv'w+uvw' \)
6 \( (f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x) \)
Tangent equation

\( y = y(x_{0}) + y'(x_{0})(x-x_{0}) \)

Antiderivative

# \( f(x) \) \( F(x) \)

2 \( k \) \( kx \)
3 \( x \) \( \frac{x^2}{2} \)
4 \( x^n \) \( \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \)
5 \( \frac{1}{x} \) \( \ln|x| \)
6 \( e^x \) \( e^x \)
7 \( a^x \) \( \frac{a^x}{\ln{a}} \)
8 \( \sin{x} \) \( -\cos{x} \)
9 \( \cos{x} \) \( \sin{x} \)
10 \( \frac{1}{\cos^2{x}} \) \( \tan{x} \)
11 \( \frac{1}{\sin^2{x}} \) \( -\cot{x} \)
12 \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}} \) \( \arcsin{\frac{x}{a}} \)
13 \( \frac{1}{a^2+x^2} \) \( \frac{1}{a} \arctan{\frac{x}{a}} \)
14 \( \frac{1}{a^2-x^2} \) \( \frac{1}{2a} \ln{\frac{a+x}{a-x}} \)
15 \( \frac{1}{1+x^2} \) \( \arctan{x} \)
16 \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \) \( \arcsin{x} \)
17 \( \frac{1}{\sin{x}} \) \( \ln{|\tan{\frac{x}{2}}|} \)
18 \( \frac{1}{\cos{x}} \) \( \ln{|\tan(\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\pi}{4})|} \)
19 \( \tan{x} \) \( -\ln{|\cos{x}|} \)
20 \( \cot{x} \) \( \ln{|\sin{x}|} \)
Integration rules
# Rule
1 \( \int kf(x)\, \mathrm{d}x = k \int f(x)\, \mathrm{d}x \)
2 \( \int f(kx+b)\, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{k}f(kx+b)+c \)
3 \( \int uv'\, \mathrm{d}x = uv - \int vu'\, \mathrm{d}x \)
Definite integral

\( \int_a^b f(x)\, \mathrm{d}x = F(b)-F(a) \)
