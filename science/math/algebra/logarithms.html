Logarithms

Notatitons

\( \lg{n} = \log_{2}{n} \) (binary logarithm)

\( \ln{n} = \log_{e}{n} \) (natural logarithm)

\( \lg^{k}{n} = (\lg{n})^k \) (exponentiation)

\( \lg{\lg{n}} = \lg{(\lg{n})} \) (composition)

\( a = b^{\log_{b}{a}} \)

\( \log_{c}{(ab)} = \log_{c}{a} + \log_{c}{b} \)

\( \log_{b}{a^n} = n \log_{b}{a} \)

\( \log_{b}{a} = \frac{\log_{c}{a}}{\log_{c}{b}} \)

\( \log_{b}{(1/a)} = -\log_{b}{a} \)

\( \log_{b}{a} = \frac{1}{\log_{a}{b}} \)

\( a^{\log_{b}{c}} = c^{\log_{b}{a}} \)

for all real a > 0, b > 0, c > 0, and n, where, in each equation above, logarithm bases are not 1.
