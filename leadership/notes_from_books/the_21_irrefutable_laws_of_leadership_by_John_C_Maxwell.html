The 21 irrefutable laws of leadership

by John C. Maxwell

1. The Law of the Lid – Leadership Ability Determines a Person’s Level of Effectiveness

Leadership ability is the lid that determines a person’s level of effectiveness. Your leadership ability always determines your effectiveness and the potential impact of your organization.

Leadership has a multiplying effect. By raising your leadership ability you can multiply your original effectiveness by 5 times.

Smart and talented people can't go so far because of the limitations of their leadership. To reach the highest level of effectiveness, you have to raise your leadership lid.
